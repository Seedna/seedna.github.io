About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate indie game developer dedicated to creating unique and immersive gaming experiences. Through Glowing Potion, I blend creativity with technical skills to brew magical worlds for players to explore.

With a background in both programming and game design, I handle all aspects of game development - from concept to code to pixel art. My games often feature whimsical themes, engaging mechanics, and a touch of the unexpected.

When I'm not developing games, you can find me playing indie titles, experimenting with new game engines, or enjoying a good cup of tea while sketching out new ideas.
